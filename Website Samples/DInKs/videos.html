  • Digital Interactive Keys: Redesigning the Keyboard for Creative Design
  • Digital Interactive Keys (DI N K S ): a design that takes advantage of the affordances of handheld interactive objects as a complement to touch screens for creative composition. This design enables tangible interaction with elements of a composition along with a larger touch sensitive display for interacting with the whole composition. We describe design precedents and their affordances for combining smaller physical elements with larger display devices and show how this design is unique in using handheld interactive objects for spatial exploration of elements in a composition that is synthesized on a touch screen.
