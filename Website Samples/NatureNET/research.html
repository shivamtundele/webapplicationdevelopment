RESEARCH GOAL

The research goals of the NatureNet project are to develop a citizen science platform that crowd sources observations from nature preserves and design ideas for shaping the citizen science experience.



Research Question 1. Crowdsourcing interaction design for citizen science virtual organizations. What are the roles and tasks of the crowd in a design process that engages the public in the interaction design for a virtual organization?



Research Question 2. Motivating participation in citizen science. Does crowdsourcing the design of interactive social technology for a citizen science organization motivate participation in collecting and sharing biodiversity data?
